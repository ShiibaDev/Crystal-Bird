Errors

Every error appear in raw on the CLI environmet of the administrator pc, the administrator pc is the owner of this and the company pc, if the company don't give the owner an warning or a letter to the owner Email, probably is because a connection error or a company fail ocurred.

When the red light turn on and blink, means the conection is lost or the password is wrong, the owner can solve this by itself just entering it on the Arduino Cloud dashboard.
